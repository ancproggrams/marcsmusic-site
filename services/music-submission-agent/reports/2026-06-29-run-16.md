## Verification summary

- Beatport Greenroom was verified as an active artist/label opportunity hub with artist, label, demo-management, distribution, Hype, Ampsuite, editorial and promotion paths.
- LabelRadar was verified through Beatport Greenroom as an active demo-submission and label-discovery platform with 300k+ artists and 3.6k+ labels.
- DropTrack was verified as an active artist/label promotion platform for targeted submissions to DJs, labels, playlist curators, blogs and radio with analytics and contact-list tooling.
- Promoly was verified as an active DJ-promo and tastemaker-network platform for upload, campaign creation, feedback collection and real-time tracking.
- Play MPE was verified as an active global radio/industry promotion route for radio promoters, labels, managers and artists using Caster, Player and MTR.
- AirplayAccess was verified as an active public radio-servicing route with submit/upload calls-to-action, published pricing and radio-programmer distribution.
- MusicHub was verified as an active registration-based distribution route with Beatport distribution, release planning and artist resources.
- No public business email was captured or stored for any new entry because the discovered routes are account-, campaign-, upload-, payment- or partner-onboarding based rather than public email submission routes.
