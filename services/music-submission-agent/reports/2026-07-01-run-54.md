## Opportunities added

### 1. WRSU 88.7FM Rutgers Music Director Submissions

- Official route: `https://wrsu.org/contact-us/`
- Method: official public Music Director email and snail-mail route for artist, label and promoter submissions.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: WRSU routes artist, label and promoter submissions exclusively to the Music Director. Email contact action, music-link or file packaging, metadata, rights, clean/explicit status, local/live-show fit, station fit and public-contact validation require manual review.

### 2. WHUS 91.7FM UConn New Music Committee

- Official route: `https://whus.org/new-music-committee/`
- Method: official public New Music Committee and Music Director email route for promoter album review.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: WHUS New Music Committee listens to brand new releases and reviews albums sent by promoters. Email contact action, album-versus-single fit, metadata, rights, clean/explicit status, station fit and public-contact validation require manual review.

### 3. WESU 88.1FM Middletown Music Directors

- Official route: `https://www.wesufm.org/contact-us/`
- Method: official public Music Director email route for station music submissions with links-only instruction.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: WESU instructs artists who want to submit music to contact the Music Directors and send links, not files. Link preparation, no-attachment compliance, metadata, rights, clean/explicit status, station fit and public-contact validation require manual review.

### 4. WXYC 89.3FM UNC Music Department

- Official route: `https://wxyc.org/`
- Method: official public Music Department mailing-address and station-contact route for freeform-radio consideration.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: WXYC publishes a Music Department mailing route and general station contact for its student-run freeform radio operation. Physical-versus-email routing, package preparation, metadata, rights, clean/explicit status, station fit and contact validation require manual review.

### 5. WUOG 90.5FM Athens Submit Music

- Official route: `https://wuog.org/contact/`
- Method: official public Submit Music route with Music Director and Local Music Director review.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: WUOG asks artists to submit albums for airtime consideration to the Music Directors and Local Music Directors. Album/package selection, physical-versus-email routing, metadata, rights, eligibility against WUOG airtime rules, clean/explicit status and station fit require manual review.
