## Opportunities added

### 1. KCSB-FM Santa Barbara Artist Inquiry

- Official route: `https://www.kcsb.org/contact/artist-inquiry/`
- Method: official public artist inquiry form for radio music-library, live-air, station-event and premiere consideration.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: KCSB routes artists, bands and DJs to an external Google Form for live on-air performance, station events, music-library addition and KCSB.org premieres. External-form handling, route selection, rights, metadata, clean/explicit status, artist-fit and form-field validation require manual review.

### 2. WNCW 88.7 Submit Music

- Official route: `https://www.wncw.org/submit-music`
- Method: official public Programming Department physical-mail and digital-download email route.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: WNCW prefers CD submissions but also allows WAV/MP3 files by public digital-file email via Dropbox or Google Drive. Physical-versus-digital routing, one-sheet/EPK packaging, file-link preparation, metadata, rights, clean/explicit status, station fit and contact validation require manual review.

### 3. WYEP Pittsburgh Area Music Submissions

- Official route: `https://www.wyep.org/wyep-pittsburgh-area-music-submissions`
- Method: official public local-music email and hard-copy route for airplay and local-feature consideration.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: WYEP is focused on Pittsburgh/Western Pennsylvania artists and accepts links by local-music email or hard-copy mail. Regional eligibility, specialty-show targeting, FCC-clean edits, track-pick guidance, metadata, rights, station fit and contact validation require manual review.

### 4. KUTX Austin Submit Your Music

- Official route: `https://kutx.org/submit-your-music/`
- Method: official public Music Director email route with WAV download link and specialty-show targeting.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: KUTX requires Music Director email packaging with a streaming link, downloadable WAV link, bio/release notes and optional specialty-show targeting. WAV-link preparation, ATX/TX/local flagging, FCC-clean radio edit, mastered-track status, specialty-show fit, metadata, rights and contact validation require manual review.

### 5. KXT 91.7 Dallas Music Submissions

- Official route: `https://kxt.org/submissions/`
- Method: official public digital music submission form for airplay consideration.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: KXT uses a Wufoo online form and requires one-song-only consideration, streaming link, downloadable WAV file and authorization for KXT to air selected music. External-form handling, WAV specs, one-song selection, rights authorization, metadata, station fit and form-field validation require manual review.
