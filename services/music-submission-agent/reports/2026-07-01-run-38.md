## Verification notes

- SXSW was queued from the official 2027 Music Festival Showcase application route. It is paid and deadline-sensitive, so it remains manual.
- The New Colossus Festival was queued from the official 2027 artist application route. Its page lists a non-refundable fee, eligibility restrictions and a no-applications-by-email rule, so it remains manual.
- BBC Music Introducing was queued as a UK uploader route. The official BBC page was protected from automated fetching during research; the route was cross-checked through public reference material and kept manual because it requires registration, postcode routing, UGC terms and locality review.
- triple j Unearthed was queued from official ABC help pages. The route requires ABC account/profile setup and Australian citizen/resident eligibility, so it remains manual.
- ccMixter was queued as a Creative Commons remix/upload community. It requires owner-approved license and source-material review before any stems, a cappellas or remixes are released.
