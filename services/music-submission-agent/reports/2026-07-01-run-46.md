## Opportunities added

### 1. WREK Atlanta Music Submissions

- Official route: `https://www.wrek.org/music/`
- Method: official public Music Director email route plus physical media route.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: WREK accepts music by email or physical mail, but programming is freeform and DJ-selected, so media format, metadata, clean-content notes, station/show fit and no-response expectations require manual review.

### 2. KFJC 89.7FM For Musicians

- Official route: `https://kfjc.org/for-musicians/`
- Method: official public physical-media-only route to the Music Director.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: KFJC says it can only accept physical media and cannot accommodate digital-only submissions yet; the related contact flow includes a no-robots control, so physical packaging, no-return terms, format fit and anti-bot-safe handling remain manual.

### 3. WMBR 88.1 FM MIT Your Music

- Official route: `https://wmbr.org/www/md`
- Method: official public physical CD route to the Music Director.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: WMBR asks artists to send tangible physical music rather than MP3s, uses a freeform DJ-selected programming model and exposes image-protected follow-up contacts that were not decoded, so packaging, one-sheet, show fit and protected-contact handling require manual review.

### 4. KSPC 88.7FM Music Submissions

- Official route: `https://kspc.org/contact/music-submissions/`
- Method: official public album/EP email-download-link and physical-mail route.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: KSPC accepts albums or EPs by mail or email/cloud download link but rejects singles, demos, non-downloadable streaming links, iTunes/Spotify links, cassettes and substantially AI-generated work, so eligibility, originality, file access, FCC-clean status and metadata require manual review.

### 5. KXLU 88.9 FM Los Angeles Music Submissions

- Official route: `https://kxlu.com/contact/`
- Method: official public physical-mail routes to Music Director, Demolisten and A Fistful of Vinyl.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: KXLU requires FCC-clean submissions and has separate physical routing paths for general submissions, demos and punk/folk/vinyl, so clean-radio status, routing choice, physical packaging, metadata and station-fit checks remain manual.
