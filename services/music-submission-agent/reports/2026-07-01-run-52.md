## Opportunities added

### 1. SomaFM Submit Music for Airplay

- Official route: `https://somafm.com/contact/submitmusic.html`
- Method: official public digital music submission email route with channel-specific Music Director routing.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: SomaFM requires channel-specific music director routing and digital delivery via Bandcamp YUM/download code or WeTransfer/Dropbox link. No-AI policy, channel fit, MP3 bitrate/metadata, no-SoundCloud/YouTube/Mixcloud files, duplicate-email avoidance, rights, clean/explicit status and contact validation require manual review.

### 2. KVRX 91.7FM Austin Music Submissions

- Official route: `https://kvrx.org/app/contact/`
- Method: official public Music Department email route with physical and digital submission support.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: KVRX routes airplay submissions to the Music Department address and says physical and digital submissions are accepted. AI-policy compliance, physical-versus-digital routing, metadata, rights, clean/explicit status, station fit and contact validation require manual review.

### 3. WUSB 90.1FM Stony Brook Music Director Submissions

- Official route: `https://wusb.fm/node/78380`
- Method: official public Music Director email and physical mail route.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: WUSB accepts music by Music Director email or station mail. Email-versus-physical routing, metadata, rights, clean/explicit status, station/show fit, address handling and contact validation require manual review.

### 4. KSCU 103.3FM Santa Clara Music Director Submissions

- Official route: `https://kscu.org/about/`
- Method: official public Music Director email route for airplay consideration.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: KSCU provides a Music Director address for airplay submissions. Outreach packaging, metadata, rights, clean/explicit status, file/link preparation, station fit and public-contact validation require manual review.

### 5. KZSC 88.1FM Santa Cruz Genre Music Directors

- Official route: `https://kzsc.org/contact/`
- Method: official public genre Music Director contact route with protected email links.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: KZSC lists Station Music Directors by genre, including RPM & Electronic and International, but extracted email links are protected/redacted. Authorized-contact handling without decoding, genre-director selection, metadata, rights, clean/explicit status and station fit require manual review.
