## Opportunities added

### 1. WPRB 103.3 FM Princeton Music Submissions

- Official route: `https://wprb.com/music-submissions/`
- Method: official public Music Director email and physical-media route with physical priority.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: WPRB accepts physical releases and a public Music Director email route, but physical submissions are prioritized. Media format, metadata, clean/explicit notes, rights, station fit and digital-versus-physical route choice require manual review.

### 2. KZSU Stanford 90.1 FM Music Directors

- Official route: `https://kzsu.stanford.edu/contact/`
- Method: official public Music Director email and snail-mail route with a cookie-protected music-guidelines page.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: KZSU routes music to Music Directors and snail mail, while the linked music-guidelines page requires cookies/session handling. Guideline access, director targeting, metadata, rights, media choice and anti-spam-safe contact handling require manual review.

### 3. WCBN-FM Ann Arbor Music Hard-Copy Submissions

- Official route: `https://wcbn.org/contact/`
- Method: official public hard-copy album route with Music Director and genre-director routing.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: WCBN accepts music from musicians willing to send hard copies, preferably CD or vinyl, and uses genre-specific director routing. Physical package preparation, genre routing, metadata, rights, station fit and protected/masked email handling require manual review.

### 4. WHRB 95.3 FM Harvard Radio Promo Submissions

- Official route: `https://www.whrb.org/contact`
- Method: official public department-addressed physical CD or LP promo-submission route.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: WHRB says promo music requests should use physical CDs or LPs addressed to the appropriate department or member. Department fit, package preparation, metadata, rights, clean-content status and misrouting checks require manual review.

### 5. Soundplate Free Playlist Submission System

- Official route: `https://play.soundplate.com/`
- Method: official public free Spotify and Deezer playlist submission grid with curator selection.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: Soundplate Play offers free playlist submissions, but track URL readiness, playlist relevance, curator choice, anti-spam compliance, rights, metadata and downstream form/session steps require manual review.
