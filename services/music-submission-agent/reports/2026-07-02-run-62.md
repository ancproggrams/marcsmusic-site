## Opportunities added

### 1. WFMU Send Us Music

- Official route: `https://wfmu.org/sendmusic.html`
- Method: official public WFMU Send Us Music physical materials route for Music Director review.
- Pricing: free route; physical mailing costs are external/manual.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: physical mailing, release/package choice, metadata, rights/originality notes, clean-language context and station-fit checks must remain manual.

### 2. Bandcamp for Artists

- Official route: `https://bandcamp.com/artists`
- Method: official public Bandcamp for Artists signup route to create an artist store and release digital or physical music.
- Pricing: freemium/manual; artist accounts are free, with revenue share and optional Pro tier.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: account creation, music/catalog upload, pricing, payout/tax setup, metadata, artwork, rights/originality and optional Pro decisions must remain manual.

### 3. Jamendo Artists Services

- Official route: `https://artists.jamendo.com/`
- Method: official public Jamendo Artists Services route for Jamendo Music, licensing, in-store placement, Content ID and promotion.
- Pricing: freemium/manual.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: account access, upload/catalog activation, licensing opt-in/out, collecting-society compatibility, Content ID, metadata and rights/originality checks must remain manual.

### 4. Newgrounds Audio Portal

- Official route: `https://www.newgrounds.com/audio`
- Method: official public Newgrounds Audio Portal account route for music/audio submissions and community discovery.
- Pricing: free/manual; supporter upsell exists but is not required for queuing.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: account creation, upload form handling, category/rating selection, rights/originality, explicit-content labeling and portal-fit checks must remain manual.

### 5. SoundCloud Upload Your Music

- Official route: `https://soundcloud.com/upload`
- Method: official public SoundCloud upload route with free upload allowance and optional Pro plan upgrade.
- Pricing: freemium/manual.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: account/login, audio upload, metadata, artwork, public/private settings, rights/originality, explicit/content settings and optional paid-plan decisions must remain manual.
