# Music Submission Agent - Run 15

Date: 2026-06-29

## Scope

Discover, verify, enrich, deduplicate and queue legitimate global music submission opportunities for MarcsMusic using only public authorized routes.

This run focused on electronic-friendly label distribution, DJ-promo, radio-promotion and independent label-services routes that were not already present in the seed pipeline.

## Verification notes

- LabelWorx was verified as an active electronic label distribution and promo platform with public signup and personal review of every request.
- iGroove was verified as an active account-based platform covering distribution, release creation, royalty splits, marketing services and playlist pitching.
- AirPlay Direct was verified as an active global radio-promotion route with artist signup, DPK features, radio tastemaker reach and paid annual account tiers.
- Inflyte was verified as an active promo-delivery platform for labels, agencies and artists, with DJ/tastemaker delivery, pre-saves, airplay monitoring, pricing and a quick-form intake.
- HAULIX was verified as an active secure promo-page and Media Connect platform for artists, labels and publicists.
- ZIPDJ was verified as an active DJ pool with a public artist/label music submission form, MP3/ZIP upload field, submission terms and broad electronic genre coverage.
- Kudos Distribution was verified as an active independent music distributor with physical/digital distribution, playlist pitching, a label portal and public apply route.
- Republic of Music was verified as an active independent distribution and label-services company with a public contact route for distribution, label management, sync, label services, publishing and manufacturing.
