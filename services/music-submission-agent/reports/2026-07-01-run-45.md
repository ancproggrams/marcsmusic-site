## Opportunities added

### 1. CJSW 90.9 FM Calgary Music Submissions

- Official route: `https://cjsw.com/music/submit/`
- Method: official public digital album/EP upload form via external Jotform.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: CJSW only accepts recent digital album submissions with at least three unique tracks, MP3 ZIP packaging and no singles, so release age, file format, metadata, external form controls and station fit require manual review.

### 2. CFUV 101.9 FM Victoria Music Submissions

- Official route: `https://cfuv.uvic.ca/music-submissions/`
- Method: official public Music Director email/download-link route.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: CFUV requires at least three unique tracks, 320kbps MP3 download links, bio and proper artist/album/track metadata, followed by freeform music-team fit review.

### 3. CJSR 88.5 FM Edmonton Submit Music

- Official route: `https://www.cjsr.com/submit-music/`
- Method: official public album/EP digital-email and physical-mail route.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: CJSR accepts albums or EPs only, rejects paid streaming links and singles, requires metadata/language warnings and publishes a January 2026 AI policy that rejects wholly AI-generated music.

### 4. KBOO 90.7 FM Portland Submit Music

- Official route: `https://kboo.fm/submit-your-music`
- Method: official public physical-mail route to genre-specific reviewers.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: KBOO says it does not yet have a digital library and cannot download/burn CDs of digital submissions, so package prep, genre addressee choice, no-return/no-airplay terms and physical routing remain manual.

### 5. KOOP 91.7 FM Austin Music Library Submissions

- Official route: `https://koop.org/about-us/contact/`
- Method: official public Music Library email route.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: KOOP publishes music@koop.org as its Music Library submission route, but does not publish a complete static ingest workflow, so pitch scope, file/link preference, content notes, metadata and email validation require human review.
