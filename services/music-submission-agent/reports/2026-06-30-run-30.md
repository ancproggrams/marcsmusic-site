## Verification notes

- WTJU publishes a public Submit Music page, accepts CD, LP/45 and digital MP3/WAV/FLAC files, and says streaming links are fine for review but not for broadcast-library ingestion.
- WUVT publishes 2026-2027 staffheads and a Music Director contact route, but explicitly does not accept digital music submissions.
- KXCI publishes Music Department submission recommendations, accepts worldwide music, lists MP3/WAV download links and physical routes, and requires review for genre, quality and explicit content.

## Dedupe notes

- Added routes were checked against prior run22-run29 seed additions by canonical platform name, website URL and submission URL.
- No duplicate of previously queued public submission routes was added in this run.
- Ambiguous, inactive, account-only, payment-only or insufficiently verified candidates were excluded from this batch.

## Worker rules applied

- Public authorized routes only.
- Do not bypass CAPTCHA, login, anti-bot, JavaScript, protected email or payment controls.
- Mark manual review when the route requires physical mailing, human targeting, account login, payment, unclear rights terms, protected forms or explicit-content/originality checks.
- Do not send emails or submit forms automatically.
- Do not upload music without explicit owner approval.
