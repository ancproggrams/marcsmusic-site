## Opportunities added

### 1. BBC Music Introducing Uploader

- Official route: `https://www.bbc.co.uk/music/introducing/uploader/`
- Method: official public BBC Introducing upload portal for unsigned and under-the-radar UK music talent.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: BBC Introducing uses an account/upload flow with postcode/local-show routing, UGC terms, eligibility checks, metadata, rights/originality assertions and possible editorial routing.

### 2. Amazing Radio Artist Uploads

- Official route: `https://amazingradio.com/`
- Method: official public artist-upload route through the Amazing Radio website for emerging-artist airplay consideration.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: Amazing Radio uses a website/account upload workflow for emerging-artist airplay consideration. Upload handling, rights/originality assertions, metadata, genre fit, territory fit and editorial review require manual review.

### 3. Triple J Unearthed Artist Upload Platform

- Official route: `https://www.abc.net.au/triplejunearthed/`
- Method: official public Triple J Unearthed upload platform for independent Australian music.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: Triple J Unearthed is Australian-music focused and uses account/upload workflows. Australian eligibility, original-music status, metadata, rights, competition rules and editorial airplay review require manual review.

### 4. Audiomack Creator Upload Platform

- Official route: `https://audiomack.com/upload`
- Method: official public creator upload route for direct artist music hosting and discovery.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: Audiomack requires account/login and upload actions. Rights/originality assertions, metadata, clean/explicit flags, content-policy review and any promotion/monetization choices require manual review.

### 5. SoundOn Music Distribution and Artist Promotion

- Official route: `https://www.soundon.global/`
- Method: official public SoundOn artist signup and music distribution route for TikTok and partner platforms.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: SoundOn requires account/signup and release/distribution setup. Country availability, distribution terms, royalty-share model, rights/originality assertions, metadata, platform choices and payout/legal settings require manual review.
