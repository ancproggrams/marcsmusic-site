## Verification and enrichment notes

- WMSE: official Music Department page accepts CD, vinyl, digital and cassette submissions and lists a public Music Director route. Manual review is needed for FCC-clean checks, asset preparation and human outreach.
- KCSB: official Artist Inquiry route covers live on-air, events, music library, premieres and video/song consideration through an external form. Manual review is needed because the form is external and may include validation or anti-spam controls.
- CJSR: official Submit Music page accepts digital albums/EPs by email and physical submissions, rejects paid streaming-only links and singles, and includes separate paid advertising. Paid promotion remains blocked.
- CFUV: official Music Submissions guidelines require at least three unique tracks, 320kbps MP3 download links, biography and tracklisting; physical submissions are restricted mostly to local/special cases.
- Radio Western: official temporary page states the server is damaged but explicitly directs new music submissions and promotion to the music address rather than the general address. Manual review is needed because the site is degraded and detailed asset policy is not visible.
