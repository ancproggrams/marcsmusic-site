# Music Submission Discovery Run 7

## Scope

Discover, verify, enrich, deduplicate, and queue legitimate global music submission, release, distribution, licensing, and promotion opportunities for MarcsMusic using only publicly available authorized routes.

No automated form submission was attempted.

This run added 8 new public-authorized artist upload/distribution/licensing routes to the persistent seed pipeline:

## Verification notes

- RouteNote was verified as an active digital music distribution service with global artist reach, online distribution, analytics, marketing tools, and royalty handling.
- Amuse was verified as an active global music company offering self-service digital music distribution and artist services.
- LANDR was verified as an active music creation platform with music distribution services to DSPs and aggregators.
- TuneCore was verified as an active independent digital music distribution, publishing, and licensing service.
- DistroKid was verified as an active independent distribution service for musicians and rights holders, with music and music-video distribution features.
- CD Baby was verified as an active independent digital music distributor after its physical media distribution was discontinued.
- Ditto Music was verified as an active worldwide online music distribution company with label services and promotional services.
- BeatStars was verified as an active global music licensing marketplace for producers and recording artists.
