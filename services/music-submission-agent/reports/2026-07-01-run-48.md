## Opportunities added

### 1. WMSE 91.7FM Milwaukee Music Department

- Official route: `https://wmse.org/about-wmse-radio/music-department/`
- Method: official public physical mail/walk-in and digital-download route to Music Director.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: WMSE accepts CD, vinyl, cassette and digital-download submissions, but every item must be reviewed for quality, content, FCC-clean tracks, metadata, format choice, Music Director routing and DJ/program fit before any contact, file link or physical package action.

### 2. KRUI 89.7FM Get Your Music Played

- Official route: `https://krui.fm/about/music/`
- Method: official public Music Director email route with tracklist and clean/explicit-content notes.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: KRUI asks artists to send music by email and include tracklist and swearing/obscenity information, so clean/explicit flags, remote/physical-media guidance, metadata, rights, station fit and no-response expectations require manual review.

### 3. KXCI 91.3 Tucson Music Department Submissions

- Official route: `https://kxci.org/about/music-department/`
- Method: official public email/download-link and physical route to Music Department.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: KXCI accepts music from around the world but prioritizes Tucson/Arizona artists, reviews music through staff/volunteers and requires metadata, explicit-language notes, bio/social/contact details and MP3/WAV download links or physical copies. The web extract rendered the email as protected/redacted, so no protected contact was decoded.

### 4. WXDU 88.7FM Duke Radio Music Submissions

- Official route: `https://www.wxdu.org/contact.html`
- Method: official public airplay-submission email route with Bandcamp YUM-code preference.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: WXDU asks submitters to use Bandcamp YUM codes where possible and routes music through Music Director categories, so download-code choice, rights, metadata, RPM/world/local category routing, station fit and email-send approval require manual review.

### 5. KDVS 90.3FM Davis Music Department Physical Submissions

- Official route: `https://kdvs.org/contact`
- Method: official public Music Department contact and physical-submission-only route.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: KDVS lists Music Directors for music review, library maintenance, mail sorting and record-label contact, and instructs that all new music should be sent to the mailing address as physical submissions only. Package preparation, media format, metadata, rights, station fit and avoiding digital workarounds require manual review.
