## Opportunities added

### 1. CJSF 90.1FM Burnaby Music CD Submissions

- Official route: `https://www.cjsf.ca/contact-us/submission`
- Method: official public Music Coordinator email and physical CD submission route.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: CJSF routes music directly to the Music Coordinator by email or mail. Physical-versus-digital routing, metadata, release format, rights, radio clean/explicit status, station fit and contact validation require manual review.

### 2. CHLY 101.7FM Nanaimo Submit Your Music

- Official route: `https://www.chly.ca/submissions`
- Method: official public submit-music form with digital and physical submission guidelines.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: CHLY accepts physical and digital submissions but requires a public form workflow, no-AI policy review, high-quality MP3/AAC requirements, explicit-language flags, local/CANCON/diversity metadata, rights and library-fit review.

### 3. CFRU 93.3FM Guelph Music Department Submissions

- Official route: `https://www.cfru.ca/music/`
- Method: official public Music Director download-link route with local music submission form.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: CFRU accepts EP or album submissions via download links and exposes a local-only submission form. Album/EP minimum-track eligibility, local-versus-nonlocal routing, download-link packaging, metadata, rights and station fit require manual review.

### 4. CKUT 90.3FM Montreal Submit Music

- Official route: `https://ckut.ca/submit-music-to-ckut/`
- Method: official public Music Department email and physical-media route with no-singles policy.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: CKUT accepts physical media and 320 MP3 digital submissions, but no-singles and minimum-three-distinct-tracks rules, physical-versus-digital routing, library criteria, metadata, rights, programmer-fit and clean/explicit status require manual review.

### 5. CJAM 99.1FM Windsor Digital Music Submissions

- Official route: `https://www.cjam.ca/submit/`
- Method: official public digital-only Music Director email route with download-link requirements.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: CJAM has moved to digital-only submissions, but four-or-more-original-tracks rule, no-Spotify-links requirement, download-link preparation, ID3 metadata, EPK/release info, rights, file quality and station fit require manual review.
