## Verification notes

- KALX was queued from the official Music Directors route. It remains manual because the station requires professionally pressed physical CDs or LPs and explicitly rejects digital submissions.
- KEXP was queued from the official Music Director / Music Department rotation-consideration route. It remains manual because email outreach requires curated track selection, clean-edit checks, rights verification, metadata and tone/context approval.
- Monstercat was queued from the official demo-submission section that links to Uncaged, Instinct and Silk demo routes on LabelRadar. It remains manual because an external submission/login flow, originality review and label-specific genre targeting are required.
- Spinnin Records was queued from the official Talent Pool route. It remains manual because demo submission requires login/account access and community/talent-pool terms review.
- Armada Music was queued from the official Demo drop navigation route. It remains manual because the complete dynamic submission workflow was not safely visible from the fetched static page and must be inspected without bypassing platform controls.
