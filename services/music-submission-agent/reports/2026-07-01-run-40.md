## Verification notes

- KVRX was queued from its official public contact page. It remains manual because the route accepts physical and digital submissions, but track choice, clean-edit status, release metadata, pitch copy and AI-assisted/generated-material policy review require owner approval.
- WNYU was queued from its official Send us music route. It remains manual because physical media are prioritized and the digital Music Director route still requires curated outreach, rights verification and metadata review.
- KZSC was queued from its official Station Music Directors contact page. It remains manual because the relevant genre directors are exposed through protected links, which were not decoded or bypassed.
- WHPK was queued from its official artist/label routing guidance. It remains manual because submissions should be routed to relevant format chiefs or music directors, not the station-manager route, and no generated contacts were added.
- WNUR was queued from its official Submit Your Sound section. It remains manual because the primary route is an external Google Form plus a digital-files mailbox, requiring rights, file and session-control review.

## Dedupe and exclusion notes

No duplicate platform keys were added in this run. Repository search did not find existing KVRX, WNYU, KZSC, WHPK or WNUR seed entries. Additional researched routes were excluded when an authorized public submission route could not be verified, when the route was not relevant enough for MarcsMusic, or when a page could not be safely inspected without platform controls.
