## Opportunities added

### 1. KEXP Seattle Music Director Airplay Consideration

- Official route: `https://www.kexp.org/about/submission-guidelines/`
- Method: official public Music Director email route with digital download guidelines.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: KEXP routes airplay consideration to the Music Director address, but freeform email outreach, streaming and downloadable WAV links, release metadata, clean edit/lyrics, rights, DJ/show targeting, station fit and contact validation require manual review.

### 2. WXPN 88.5 Music Director and Local Show Submissions

- Official route: `https://xpn.org/contact/`
- Method: official public Music Director and Local Show email/download-link route.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: WXPN requests an EPK with a digitally downloadable music link for airplay and a separate Local Show route with WAV/high-quality MP3 download links. EPK completeness, local-versus-station-wide routing, file quality, rights, metadata and radio fit require manual review.

### 3. KALX 90.7FM Berkeley Physical Music Submissions

- Official route: `https://kalx.berkeley.edu/about/contact/`
- Method: official public physical CD/LP Music Directors route with no-digital-submissions policy.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: KALX only accepts physical professionally pressed CDs or LPs and rejects downloads, file transfers, streaming links, CD-Rs and other digital forms. Physical-media preparation, mailing, metadata, rights, station fit and clean/explicit status require manual review.

### 4. UKF / Pilot Submit Your Demo

- Official route: `https://ukf.com/submit-your-demo/`
- Method: official public demo submission form for UKF or Pilot release consideration.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: UKF uses a public demo form and warns not to send demos to the support address. Form workflow, track upload/link handling, bass-genre fit, rightsholder/licensing distinction, metadata and release-readiness require manual review.

### 5. KFAI Minneapolis Music Department and Music Library

- Official route: `https://kfai.org/about/contact/`
- Method: official public Music Department and Music Library contact route.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: KFAI says artists can share music with its Music Department and Music Library by email, but the address is protected/redacted in the page extraction. Authorized-contact handling, email validation, metadata, rights, diverse-programming fit, file/link preparation and clean/explicit status require manual review.
