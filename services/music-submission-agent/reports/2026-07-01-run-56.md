## Opportunities added

### 1. KRCL 90.9FM Salt Lake City How to Submit Music

- Official route: `https://krcl.org/about/how-to-submit-music/`
- Method: official public KRCL Music Department submission route with physical CD instructions and a Utah-local Google Form digital option.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: physical CD submissions, the Utah-local Google Form upload route, no-demo/no-CD-R restrictions, station/DJ fit, release metadata and ownership/rights packaging must be checked manually.

### 2. Radio Milwaukee 88Nine Music Submission

- Official route: `https://radiomilwaukee.org/music-submission`
- Method: official public Radio Milwaukee submission requirements for digital WAV review.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: WAV-preferred audio, radio edits for explicit tracks, lyrics and artist-info document preparation, no-physical-media restriction, rights/metadata checks and station fit require manual review before any submission.

### 3. WNXP 91.1FM Nashville Music Submissions

- Official route: `https://wnxp.org/music-submissions/`
- Method: official public WNXP email route for clean mastered WAV download-link airplay consideration.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: WNXP asks for a download link to a clean mastered 44.1k/16-bit stereo WAV and excludes MP3s; email sending, link selection, clean-edit status, metadata, rights and station-fit checks require manual review.

### 4. KGNU Community Radio Contact Staff or DJ Music Department

- Official route: `https://kgnu.org/contact-us/`
- Method: official public KGNU Contact Staff or DJ form with Music department option for manual station-fit routing.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: the route is a general contact form with a Music department option and reCaptcha v3. No CAPTCHA bypass or automated form submission is allowed.

### 5. WFUV New York Send Music to Music Department

- Official route: `https://wfuv.org/contactus`
- Method: official public WFUV snail-mail route to the Music Department.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: physical-package preparation, rights/metadata, station fit, follow-up call timing and any mail/phone workflow must remain manual.
