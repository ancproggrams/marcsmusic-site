## Opportunities added

### 1. 95bFM Auckland Submit Music

- Official route: `https://95bfm.com/news/how-do-you-do-submitting-music`
- Method: official public email route with streaming link plus lossless download link.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: 95bFM asks artists to email a streaming link and lossless download link, so release-date window, WAV/FLAC access, metadata, paid-PR neutrality, station/show fit and no-feedback expectations require manual review. The public web extract redacted the address, so no protected or masked contact was decoded.

### 2. Fresh On The Net Send Us A Track

- Official route: `https://freshonthenet.co.uk/submit/`
- Method: official public weekly SoundCloud track submission form.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: Fresh On The Net accepts one public embeddable SoundCloud track per weekly inbox, with Terms acceptance, inbox-open timing, one-track limits, no-repeat/no-remix rules, SoundCloud permissions and AI-policy review required before any submission.

### 3. dublab Radio Promotional Materials

- Official route: `https://www.dublab.com/about`
- Method: official public promotional-materials email route.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: dublab lists a promotional-materials route, but the workflow is freeform and program/DJ-selected, so station/show fit, rights, EPK/audio-link packaging, clean/explicit notes and anti-spam-safe outreach remain manual.

### 4. NTS Radio Show Proposal

- Official route: `https://www.nts.live/contact`
- Method: official public radio-show proposal form with audio-demo links.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: the NTS route is a programmed radio-show proposal rather than a simple track inbox, and it requires human selection of proposal, show type, audio-demo links, rights status and creative fit before any form action.

### 5. WFMU Send Music to WFMU

- Official route: `https://wfmu.org/sendmusic.html`
- Method: official public physical-media route to Music Director review.
- Pricing: free-first.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: WFMU asks for physical materials for review, so media format, package preparation, one-sheet/metadata, station fit, no-return expectations and separation from unrelated login/contact routes require manual review.
