## Verification notes

- KEXP: official submission guidelines list a digital route to `md@kexp.org` and specify required release materials. Route is free, but should be handled manually to avoid spam and to ensure the one-sheet, links and clean edits are correct.
- KALX: official contact page lists `music@kalx.berkeley.edu`, but the airplay route currently accepts only physical professionally pressed CDs or LPs. Digital links/downloads are explicitly not accepted for airplay.
- WXPN: official contact page lists airplay and Local Show submission instructions, but the email links are Cloudflare-protected mailto links. They were not bypassed or scraped.
- KJHK: official submission page lists `submitmusic@kjhk.org` for electronic submissions and says to send information and music links, not attached files.
- Spinnin Records: official Talent Pool page requires account login to submit a demo. No login, upload or protected-form action was attempted.
- ISC 2026: official rules confirm online entry, MP3 upload or song-link options, entry fees, international eligibility, AI restrictions and retained ownership. It remains payment/manual-review only.

## Safety result

No accounts were created, no protected forms were submitted, no anti-bot systems were bypassed, no payment was attempted, no music was uploaded, no terms were accepted, no guessed/generated contacts were produced and no email was sent.
