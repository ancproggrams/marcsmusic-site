## Opportunities added

### 1. KUTX Submit Your Music for On-Air Rotation

- Official route: `https://kutx.org/submit-your-music/`
- Method: official KUTX Submit Your Music route for on-air rotation via `music@kutx.org`, with streaming link, WAV download link, bio/release notes and clean/FCC-ready final masters.
- Pricing: free/manual route.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: targeted email preparation, release-link selection, WAV file access, local ATX/TX subject labeling, clean/radio-edit confirmation, rights/originality assertions and KUTX programming fit must remain manual.

### 2. Radio K KUOM Submitting Music

- Official route: `https://radiok.org/submitting-music`
- Method: official Radio K Submitting Music route accepting physical releases by mail or digital releases through a Music Department email with cloud ZIP links and high-quality audio, preferably WAV/FLAC.
- Pricing: free/manual route.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: physical-vs-digital routing, cloud ZIP creation, FCC-unfriendly track labeling, local labeling, metadata, rights/originality assertions and Radio K station fit must remain manual.

### 3. KCR College Radio Music Submissions

- Official route: `https://kcr.sdsu.edu/contact/music-submissions/`
- Method: official digital-only KCR music submission route via `kcr@sdsu.edu`, requiring FCC-clean MP3 files with `Artist - Song Title` file names.
- Pricing: free/manual route.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: clean master selection, MP3 format confirmation, filename formatting, metadata, rights/originality assertions and automated playlist/library fit must remain manual.

### 4. KCSB Artist Inquiry Form

- Official route: `https://www.kcsb.org/contact/artist-inquiry/`
- Method: official KCSB Artist Inquiry route linking to an external Google Form for live on-air performance, station events/concerts, music library adds and KCSB.org premieres.
- Pricing: free/manual route.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: external form completion, possible Google validation/anti-abuse controls, route/eligibility choice, metadata, rights/originality assertions and event/library/premiere fit must remain manual.

### 5. KZSC Santa Cruz Station Music Director Contacts

- Official route: `https://kzsc.org/contact/`
- Method: official KZSC staff/contact route with public station Music Director and genre-director contacts for rock, hardcore, hip-hop, RPM/electronic, jazz/blues, folk and international music.
- Pricing: free/manual route.
- Queue status: `needs_manual_review`.
- Review reason: correct director selection, non-spam targeted pitch preparation, metadata, clean/radio-edit labeling, rights/originality assertions and KZSC programming fit must remain manual.
