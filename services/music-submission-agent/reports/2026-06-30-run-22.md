## Verification notes

- Soundplate official page states artists can submit music to Spotify and Deezer playlists by selecting relevant playlists and describes the service as free.
- DailyPlaylists official page shows a Standard Tier that lets artists submit for free, with sign-in, pricing, premium credits and a public business contact visible.
- Groover remains paid/manual-review only because its official page describes curator/pro access starting at paid credits.
- Triple J Unearthed is active on ABC with current 2026 featured artists, recent staff reviews, and electronic/dance tracks visible.
- BBC Introducing and Amazing Radio are legitimate active/public routes, but automation must not proceed beyond public research because their upload flows are account-gated.
- TIDAL Upload is marked manual review because current public reporting indicates US-only / 18+ limitations and TIDAL announced AI-generated music monetization/labeling policy changes effective around July 2026.

## Queue policy applied

- Free routes get high discovery priority.
- Freemium routes are queued lower and must keep paid upgrade paths out of automation.
- Paid / credit / campaign routes are marked manual review and cannot submit without explicit owner approval.
- CAPTCHA, login, payment, upload rights, region-gating and account-gated flows remain manual-review cases.
