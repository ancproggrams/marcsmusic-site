Styleguide

Mostly black and white, with pastel blue restraint.

The system uses hard monochrome contrast for music controls and pastel blue for active, selected and social details.

Black--ink
White--surface
Pastel blue--pastel
Blue wash--pastel-muted
Line--line
  • Type
    Editorial display, system body

    Large serif headlines give the brand a music-magazine feel while controls stay compact and readable.

  • Shape
    Eight-pixel radius

    Buttons, panels, frames and controls share one restrained radius.

  • Accent
    Pastel blue only when useful

    Blue marks playback state, selected rows and the Instagram frame shadow without turning the page into a blue theme.

  • Motion
    Native, quiet interactions

    The audio element remains available and custom controls mirror native playback state.
